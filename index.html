Our Story

At Knitted Love, we pour love into every stitch of our homemade baby blankets and play sheets, designed to bring warmth and joy to your little one. Each piece is a unique blend of style and coziness, perfect for adding beauty to any nursery. What makes our blankets truly special is that they are lovingly designed by our grandma and handstitched by underprivileged women in India. The proceeds from every sale directly contribute to their upliftment, creating a beautiful cycle of comfort and empowerment. We believe in crafting heirloom-quality blankets that not only provide comfort but also carry a touch of magic and a meaningful purpose.
